Welcome!

I hold a PhD in mathematics and am affiliated with Université du Québec à Trois-Rivières (UQTR). My work focuses on topological data analysis, especially multiparameter persistent homology, discrete Morse theory, and applications to artificial intelligence.

Research Snapshot

Here are some of my main research interests:

  • Computational topology and topological data analysis
  • Discrete Morse theory and multiparameter persistent homology
  • Explainability in deep learning
  • Topological methods for high-dimensional data and large language models
